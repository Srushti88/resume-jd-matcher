Job Title: Warehouse Operations Associate / Inventory & Logistics Executive
Job Summary
We are looking for a detail-oriented Warehouse Operations Associate to manage picking, packing, inventory handling, and warehouse cleanliness while maintaining high accuracy and productivity standards. 
The candidate should have experience working in fast-paced warehouse environments and familiarity with inventory systems, sanitation standards, and process improvement practices such as 5S, Kaizen, and Kanban.
